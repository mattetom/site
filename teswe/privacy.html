Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12/21/2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Teswe (“the App”, “we”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects user information.

Teswe is a Wear OS and Android companion application that allows users to interact with their Tesla vehicle through official Tesla APIs.

Information We Collect

Teswe does not collect or store personal data on its own servers.

The App may access the following data only after explicit user authorization:

  • Tesla account authentication tokens
  • Vehicle-related information (e.g. vehicle status, charge state, climate state)
  • Commands explicitly triggered by the user (e.g. lock/unlock, climate control)

How We Use Information

The information accessed by Teswe is used exclusively to:

  • Display vehicle status on the device
  • Execute commands requested by the user
  • Improve app stability and reliability

No data is used for advertising, profiling, or analytics purposes.

Data Storage

  • Authentication tokens are stored locally on the user’s device
  • No personal or vehicle data is stored on external servers
  • Data is never shared with third parties

Third-Party Services

Teswe interacts with:

  • Official Tesla APIs to retrieve vehicle information and send commands

Teswe does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with any third parties.

All interactions happen directly between the user’s device and Tesla services.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data, including secure local storage and encrypted communication with Tesla APIs.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

Teswe is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

User Control

  • Users can revoke Tesla access at any time from their Tesla account
  • Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
