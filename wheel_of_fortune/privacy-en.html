Privacy Policy

App: Wheel of Fortune

Last updated: January 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled in the application Wheel of Fortune (the “App”).

1. Data Controller

The data controller is the App developer.
For any privacy-related questions, you can contact: privacy@example.com

2. Personal Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All features of the App work entirely locally on the device and do not require user registration or authentication.

3. User-Provided Data

Any data or content entered by the user (such as text or settings) remains solely on the user’s device and is not sent to external servers.

4. Device Permissions

The App does not request special permissions and does not access sensitive device features such as contacts, location, camera, or microphone.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party services, including:

  • analytics services;
  • crash reporting services;
  • cloud or backup services;
  • tracking technologies;
  • in-app purchases or payment systems.

6. Data Retention

Since the App does not collect personal data, no data is stored on external servers.

7. Security

The App is designed following the principle of data minimization. By not collecting personal information, privacy and security risks are inherently reduced.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App does not collect personal data and may be used by children under the supervision of a parent or guardian, in accordance with applicable laws.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact: privacy@example.com
